Why inline styles?

  • Popular email clients like Outlook and Gmail strip out CSS <style>in the <head>.

  • Made for responsive emails, meaning your media queries are moved into the body for you so they are preserved.

  • This is the best way to guarantee your email works properly on the compatible clients listed in the docs.
